Questions

Frequently
Asked

What's included in the price?
Everything: boutique hotel accommodation, all meals, every activity on the itinerary, inter-island transfers, Balinese massages, temple ceremonies, surfing lesson, river rafting, snorkelling and cooking class. Not included: international flights, visa fees and personal travel insurance.
Do I need to ride a motorcycle?
No. The tour offers four transport options: shared motorcycle (Bike × 2), solo motorcycle (Bike × 1), self-drive car, or private car with driver. You choose what fits your comfort level.
What level of fitness is required?
Medium. The itinerary includes river rafting, surfing lessons, ridge walks and snorkelling sessions. The driving days are long but comfortable, especially in the car options. You don't need to be an athlete — just curious and ready for adventure.
What are the hotels like?
Small boutique hotels carefully selected for character and location — beachfront in Tibubiu, Bali Sea views in Depeha, glamping on lava fields in Batur and boutique stays in Ubud, the Gilis and the Nusas.
Do I need a visa for Indonesia?
Most European passport holders can enter Indonesia visa-free for up to 30 days. For longer stays a Visa on Arrival is available. Always check the latest requirements for your nationality before booking.
How does the deposit and payment work?
A $1,000 deposit secures your spot. The remaining balance is due 60 days before departure. You can pay by card, Apple Pay, Google Pay or SEPA bank transfer (Wise).
Can I join as a solo traveller?
Absolutely. Choose Bike × 1 or a car option to travel at your own pace. Solo travellers are very welcome — you'll meet the rest of the group along the way.
What is the Gili Islands horse carriage transfer?
The Gilis ban all motorised vehicles — the only transport is horse-drawn carriage (cidomo) or bicycle. Three islands, each with a completely different vibe: Trawangan for parties, Meno for honeymooners and Air for divers. Underwater statues and sunken bikes make for unforgettable snorkelling.
