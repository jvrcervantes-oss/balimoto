Questions

FAQ

What is the Bali to Komodo Challenge?
A 12-day, 11-night motorcycle adventure covering 1,259 kilometers across 6 major islands: Bali, Lombok, Sumbawa, Flores, Moyo and Komodo. Designed as a deep cultural and geological exploration, crossing the famous Wallace Line and concluding with a visit to the Komodo Dragons.
What riding experience do I need?
Recommended for riders experienced in long-distance touring and comfortable with varied conditions including heavy traffic in Bali and challenging isolated terrain in Sumbawa. Personal international travel insurance is mandatory for all participants.
What are the main highlights?
Purification ceremony at Pura Kelecung in Bali · Tambora Volcano National Park · Whale Shark snorkeling at Saleh Bay · Komodo Dragons trek · Pink Sand Beach · Beach club finale in Bali.
How is luggage transported?
Your main luggage is transported daily in a dedicated support vehicle driven by our mechanic. You only carry a daypack with essentials on your motorcycle — ensuring a comfortable and focused ride.
Which motorcycles are available?
Honda CB150X for single riders under 100kg, or the Kawasaki Versys 250 for two riders or solo riders over 100kg. All bikes subject to mandatory nightly mechanic check.
Is motorcycle insurance included?
Not included in the base price. Two options: Full-Risk Insurance policy for $282 (covers all bike damages), or a Refundable Security Deposit of $1,090 upon arrival (returned if no damages occur).
Are tour dates fixed?
Yes. Maximum of 12 motorcycles per tour and a maximum group size of 24 people. This ensures a quality, personal experience for every rider.
Can I book a call first?
Absolutely. Write to us on WhatsApp and we will schedule a free 30-minute video call so you can talk directly with our team before committing. No pressure.
